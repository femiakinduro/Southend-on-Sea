Southend-on-Sea

Southend-on-Sea is a popular seaside city located in Essex, on the northern shore of the Thames Estuary. It has long been known as a holiday destination, especially for families visiting from London. The city offers a mix of traditional seaside attractions, cultural venues, and natural beauty.

One of Southend’s most famous landmarks is Southend Pier, which is the longest pleasure pier in the world. Visitors can enjoy walking along the pier, travelling by pier train, or relaxing near the seafront. The city also has beaches, parks, arcades, and theatres, making it a lively place all year round.

Things to do in Southend-on-Sea

  • Walk along Southend Pier
  • Visit Adventure Island theme park
  • Relax on the beach
  • Explore local museums and parks
  • Visit the Forum Library
